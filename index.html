About Our Little Haven

At Treasury of Joy, we believe every child is a unique treasure. We offer a warm, secure daycare facility where children feel at home, while our nurturing environment fosters curiosity, creativity, and confidence through play-based and structured learning.

Our Enriching Programs

Little Sprouts (Ages 2-3)

Little Sprouts is an early learning program for children aged 2–3 years, focused on emotional security and exploration. Through sensory play, music, movement, stories, and free play, children develop motor skills, language, and social confidence in a safe, joyful environment.

Discovery Stars (Ages 3-4)

Discovery Stars is a play-based program for children aged 3–4 years that builds school readiness through exploration. Through stories, games, art, music, and movement, children develop early language, number sense, creativity, and social skills in a joyful environment.

Happy Learners (Ages 4-5)

Happy Learners is a play-based program for children aged 4–5 years that nurtures curiosity and expression. Through projects, role-play, art, outdoor play, and yoga, children develop language, pre-math skills, creativity, and emergent writing in a joyful environment.

Future Leaders (Ages 5-6)

Future Leaders is a school-readiness program for children aged 5–6 years, focusing on confidence, teamwork, and inquiry. Children develop early literacy, numeracy, problem-solving, and emotional skills, with writing introduced through playful, readiness-based activities.

    Inspires imagination and self-expression through art, music, dance, and open-ended creative experiences.

  • Cognitive Development

    Encourages curiosity, thinking, and problem-solving through hands-on exploration and play-based activities.

  • Socio-emotional and Ethical Learning

    Nurtures confidence, empathy, and self-regulation through relationships, collaboration, and guided interactions.

  • Physical Development

    Builds strength, coordination, and body awareness through movement, yoga, and outdoor play.

QUESTIONS?

We've Got Answers!

What age groups you accept?

We welcome children from 18 months to 5 years.

Are teachers trained in early childhood education?

Yes, all our teachers are trained in early childhood education and experienced in nurturing young learners.

What are the school hours?

Open Monday - Friday, 8 AM to 4 PM.

How do I enroll?

Visit our campus, call us for assistance or apply online today!

Is the school licensed and safe?

Yes, our playschool is fully licensed and follows all required safety and childcare guidelines.

What is your philosophy?

A play-based, child-centric approach that focus on nurturing the child—emotionally, socially, physically, and cognitively—through joyful, hands-on learning experiences.

How do teachers handle separation anxiety?

Our teachers use a gentle and caring approach to help children feel comfortable, secure, and confident at their own pace.

Are staff members background-verified?

Yes, all staff members are thoroughly background-verified to ensure a safe environment for children.

How do teachers communicate with parents?

Teachers regularly communicate with parents through updates, messages, and scheduled meetings.

What documents are required for admission?

A birth certificate, address proof, photographs, and completed admission form are required.

Is there an admission fee?

Yes, a one-time admission fee is applicable. Details are shared during enrollment.

Is fees refundable?

Fees are non-refundable. Please contact us for complete fee policy details.

Do you offer trial classes or school visits?

Yes, we encourage parents to visit the campus and attend trial classes by prior appointment.

How will I get updates about my child?

Parents receive regular updates through messages, progress reports, and parent-teacher meetings.

Can parents visit during school hours?

Yes, parents may visit during school hours with prior permission.
